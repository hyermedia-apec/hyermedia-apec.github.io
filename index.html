ABOUT

We are six students who started a project in our classroom to produce distinct and unparalleled content for our fellow audiences. Now, we're preparing to create content for a massive audience that shares our purpose.

Our group comprises Samantha Colambo, Ethan Padernal, Althea Bobadilla, France Paragas, Harriette Prestosa, and Matthew Quijano.

PROMOTIONAL STRATEGY

How we'll promote

Our promotional strategy is direct and effective. We will disseminate our promotional materials on different platforms and encourage others to share them to help expand our reach. We will also send them through APEC School's organizational email domain. We're also looking at other ways to promote, such as running non-intrusive ads and collaborating with other groups that align with our purpose.

What materials we'll use

The materials we plan on using for our promotions are:

Digital Posters
Stream Teasers
Promotional Emails through APEC Schools' Organizational Email Domain
Throwback Posts of our Recent Streams

As the previous section said, we're also looking for other promotional materials.
